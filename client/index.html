How it Works

The donation process from the time you arrive until the time you leave takes about an hour. The actual donation itself is only about 8–10 minutes — and it can save up to 3 lives.

1

Register

Create an account and set your blood group and location.

2

Get Notified

Receive notifications when someone near you needs your blood type.

3

Donate

Connect with the requester and donate blood safely at a hospital.

4

Save Lives

A single donation can save up to 3 lives. Track your impact on your dashboard.

🚨 2026 Blood Crisis

The shortage is real. Your donation is the solution.

In January 2026, the American Red Cross declared a severe blood shortage — supply fell 35% in a single month. Hospitals are running low. Surgeries are being delayed.

Every 2 seconds someone needs blood. Your blood is replaceable. A life is not.
